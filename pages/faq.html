FAQ Section

Answers to your most common questions at HZ

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
Connect to the HZ network and install the HZ Print Service through the campus portal. Access printers via your device settings and ensure you have sufficient print credits.
How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
Use the multifunction printers located throughout campus. Log in with your HZ credentials and scan documents directly to your email or cloud storage.
How can I buy something on the HZ web shop for IT events?
Access the HZ Web Shop through the student portal. Browse items, add to cart, and pay using your student account balance or accepted payment methods.
How can you book a project space?
Book project spaces through the HZ Reservation System in the student portal. Select date, time, and space type. Some spaces require faculty approval.
What are the parking instructions for the HZ facility?
Display a valid parking permit on your dashboard. Purchase passes through student services. First 30 minutes are free for visitors who register at the entrance.
